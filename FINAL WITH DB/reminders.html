Important Reminders

  1. We encourage you to visit the testing site before the scheduled examination to familiarize yourself with the campus.
  2. Application fees of high school candidates for graduation belonging to the TOP 5 of their batch (based on the third year final grading period) may be waived upon personally submitting the certification from the high school principal indicating their academic standing.
      This applies only to candidates coming from the public schools.
  3. Submitted documents become property of the UST Office for Admissions.
      a. Misrepresentation of information entered in the Application and Grades Form/Information Survey
      b. Misrepresentation during the examination
      c. Any violation of the instructions given.
  4. Visit the OFAD website (ofad.ust.edu.ph) and the Activity Log in your Freshman Admission Account every Friday for any announcement.
