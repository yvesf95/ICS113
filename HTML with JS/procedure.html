Application Procedure

  1. Click "Register".
  2. Fill out the Online Application and Grades Form and the Information Survey and press Submit. Should you wish to edit your data, you may do so by clicking "Back". Once data is successfully submitted, you can no longer edit them.
  3. Download and print the accomplished Application and Grades Form and Information Survey with your reference number found on the upper right hand corner.
  4. With your reference number, proceed to any Metrobank branch and fill out the payment slip with the following information: (Present to Metrobank the Application and Grades Form with reference number for verification purposes)
      Account Name: _____ (Your Name)
      Company Name: University of Santo Tomas
      Reference number: _____ (copy the reference number found on the accomplished Application and Grades Form)
      Telephone number: _____
      Amount: Php 500. 00
      * Applicants who wish to take the USTET in their PROVINCES should submit the accomplished Application and Grades Form and payment to the nearest Testing Center.
  5. Request the Registrar's Office of your school to accomplish the High School Grades Form. Make sure that the school principal or registrar head signs the High School Grades Form
  6. Keep a photocopy of all the documents before you proceed to step 7.
  7. For Metro Manila Applicants, forward the following documents to the OFAD personally or through any courier service (e.g. LBC, UPS, FedEx, DHL,etc.):
      7.1 Accomplished Application and Grades Form together with the Information Survey
      7.2 UST copy of your test permit with one (1) 2" x 2" picture with name and signature at the back identical to the picture stapled to the Applicant's copy.
      7.3 Clear photocopy of:
      - NSO birth certificate
      - School ID
      - Metrobank deposit slip (do not send the original copy)
  8. You may print your test permit three (3) working days after paying the non-refundable application fee (Php 500.00) at any Metrobank branch.
